I received the M.Sc. and B.Sc. degrees in Computer Science from Federal University of Pelotas (UFPel) in 2017 and 2014, respectively. I'm also a technician in Electronics from Federal Institute Sul-rio-grandense (IFSul) since 2009. Currently, I'm pursuing the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Federal University of Pelotas and the B.Eng. degree in Electrical Engineering from Federal Institute Sul-rio-grandense, acting as a student researcher at the Group of Architecture and Integrated Circuits (GACI).

Research interests

  • Electronic Design Automation (EDA);
  • Algorithms for place & route;
  • Automatic complex gate design;
  • Layout design for non-regular transistor topologies.
